Tomato Soup with Grilled Cheese Croutons!

Country originated in: Canada
Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients:
• 2 cans of tomato soup
• Cheese
• Herbs
• Bread
Directions:
1. In a large pot cook the soup from the can.
2. In a frying pan cook Grilled Cheese by buttering the outside pieces of bread and placing the cheese in the middle (not on the buttery sides)
3. Slice grilled cheese into croutons and serve on top of the soup along with an assortment of herbs.
