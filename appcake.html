Apple Cake!

Ingredients:
• 3-6 apples cut up
• 2 Tbs. of sugar (for apples)
• 1 Tbs. of cinnamon
• ¼ cups margarine or butter
• 1 cup sugar (for batter)
• 1 cup flour
• ½ cups chopped nuts (walnuts)
Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
2. Fill a pie plate with the apples
3. Sprinkle the 2 Tbs. sugar and 1 Tbs. of cinnamon over the apples.
4. In a bowl, combine the melted margarine and sugar and stir.
5. Then add in the egg and stir. Then add in the flour and stir well
6. If you are using them, sprinkle the walnuts over the apples
7. Pour the cake mixture over the apples and the walnuts
8. Bake for about 45 minutes or until golden brown
9. Serve warm with ice cream, or thick cream
