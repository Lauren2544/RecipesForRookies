Taiwanese Castella Cake!!

Country originated in: Taiwan
Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients:
- 115g cake flour
- 115g unsalted butter
- 115g milk
- 7 egg yolk
- Meringue
- 7 egg whites
- 115g sugar

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 300F. Line a 8 inch square pan with parchment paper. Prepare a baking pan that is larger then the cake pan for the water bath.
2. Melt butter and milk. Let it cool down to room temperature
3. Then sieve the cake flour. Pour the melted butter and milk into the flour.
4. Add egg yolks into the mixture one at a time.
5. Use a hand mixer or stand mixer at medium speed to beat the egg white. When itâ€™s foamy, add in sugar three times. When all the sugar is added, turn up to high speed until soft peaks form.
6. Mix 1/3 meringue into the egg yolk mixture. Then pour the egg mixture into the meringue until they are completely mixed.
7. Pour the mixture into the 8 inch baking pan. Smooth the top with the spatula. Tap the pan for a few times to remove air bubbles.
8. Put the pan on the large baking pan. Pour in hot water until it reaches half the height of the pan.
9. Bake for 65-70 minutes in the preheated oven.
10. Cool the cake for 20 minutes before serving
