Pollo Bianca

Country originated in: Italy
Yield: 2 servings

Ingredients:
- 2 tbsp. Butter
- 8 oz. Boneless chicken breast pieces
- Salt and pepper
- 1 small onion, diced
- 4 oz. Quartered mushrooms
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp. Dry white wine
- ½ cup chicken stock
- 1 to 1 ½ cups whipping cream
- 2 sprigs fresh rosemary chopped
- 1 oz. Oil-packed sundried tomatoes
- 1 ½ cups cooked fusilli pasta
- chopped fresh parsley to garnish

Directions:
1. Boil water and cook 1 ½ cups fusilli pasta
2. In a sauté pan - brown chicken pieces in hot butter. Season with salt and pepper. Add onion, cook 1 min then add mushrooms and cook 2 mins. Stir in garlic and cook 1 min.
3. Deglaze pan with white wine, add chicken stock, cream, rosemary and sundried tomatoes. Simmer gently until thickened.
4. Drain pasta and then add the cooked pasta to the sauce, add parsley and spoon into bowls. Garnish with fresh parmesan cheese. Serve immediately.
