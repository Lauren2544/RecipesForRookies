Gnocchi in Tomato Cream Sauce!

Ingredients:
- 1 pound gnocchi
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 3 teaspoons minced garlic
- 1 15-ounce can fire roasted crushed or diced tomatoes
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 1/2 teaspoon dried Italian herb blend (oregano and basil)
- 1/2 cup chicken broth or water
- 1/3 cup heavy cream
- 1/4-1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
- 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, or to taste
- fresh thyme, basil, and grated parmesan for serving optional

Directions:
1. Fill a pot with about 3 inches of water. Bring to a boil.
2. Add gnocchi, boil for 2-3 minutes until gnocchi floats. Drain, then toss gnocchi with 2 teaspoons olive oil to keep it from sticking together.
3. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and saute 1 minute until fragrant.
4. Add gnocchi and dried herbs, and saute 2-4 minutes until gnocchi begins browning on the edges.
5. Transfer gnocchi to a bowl and cover to keep warm.
6. Now make the sauce. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, and broth to the skillet over medium heat and bring to a simmer.
7. Stir in heavy cream, crushed red pepper flakes, and salt and pepper. Simmer 4-5 minutes until sauce is reduced and creamy.
8. Stir in gnocchi, taste and add salt and pepper if needed. Garnish with thyme or basil and freshly grated parmesan and serve.
