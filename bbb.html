Blueberry Boy-Bait

Country originated in: Finland
Yield: One Pan

Ingredients:
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1/3 cup butter
- 2 teaspoons double-acting baking powder
- teaspoon salt
- 2 unbeaten egg yolks
- 1 cup milk
- 2 cups drained blueberries (fresh or frozen)

Directions:
1. Set oven to 350 degrees
2. Sift together dry ingredients - 2 cups all-purpose flour and 1 Â½ cups sugar into large bowl.
3. Cut in 2/3 cup butter until particles are size of small peas. Measure 3/4 cup of mixture and reserve for crumb topping.
4. Add 2 teaspoons double-acting baking powder, 1 teaspoon salt, 2 unbeaten egg yolks and1 cup milk to remaining cake mixture in bowl.
5. In a separate bowl, beat the 2 egg whites until they are stiff but not dry. Fold gently but thoroughly into batter. Spread batter into well greased 13 x 9 inch pan. Arrange 2 cups drained blueberries (fresh or frozen) over batter. Sprinkle with reserved 3/4 cup crumb mixture.
6. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 to 50 mins. in 13 x 9 pan.
7. Serve warm or cold with whipped cream.
