Tomato Pesto Salmon and Rice Recipe

Country originated in: Canada
Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients:
- Aluminum foil
- 2 cups instant rice (you can use brown rice)
- Salmon Fillets, skins removed
- Salt and fresh ground pepper, to taste
- Olive Oil
- 1/2 cups Sundried Tomato Pesto
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 6 sprigs fresh thyme

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400F. Place rice in rice cooker and cook.
2. Lay out the foil sheets place the salmon fillets on the foil; season with salt and pepper, and rub with a drizzle of olive oil.
3. Add a tablespoon of tomato pesto on top and spread it over each fillet. Sprinkle some garlic over fillets. Place a sprig
4. Wrap up the salmon fillet and bake for 15-20 mins
