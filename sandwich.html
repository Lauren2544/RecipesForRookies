Chocolate, peanut butter, banana sandwiches!

Country originated in: Canada
Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients:
- 2 slices of bread or a wrap
- Chocolate chips
- Banana
- Peanut butter

Directions:
1. Toast the two slices of bread in a toaster (skip this step if using a wrap).
2. Spread peanut butter on the slices of bread/wrap.
3. Slice the banana in thin slices and place on bread/wrap.
4. Sprinkle chocolate chips on top of the bananas.
5. Enjoy!
